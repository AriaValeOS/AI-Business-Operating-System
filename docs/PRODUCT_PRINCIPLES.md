# Product Principles

## 1. AI First

Every repetitive business task should be executable by AI.

---

## 2. Human in Control

Humans define goals.

AI executes work.

---

## 3. Modular Everything

Every component must be replaceable.

---

## 4. One Responsibility

Every service, agent and module has exactly one responsibility.

---

## 5. Business Before Technology

Technology exists to solve business problems.

Never build technology just because it is interesting.

---

## 6. Long-term Architecture

Every architectural decision should make the platform easier to expand.

Never sacrifice architecture for short-term speed.

---

## 7. Documentation First

Important architectural decisions are documented.

---

## 8. Type Safety

No feature is complete until TypeScript passes without errors.

---

## 9. Clean UI

UI visualizes the business.

Business logic lives in services.

---

## 10. Build a Platform

Never build isolated features.

Everything becomes part of the AI Business Operating System.
