# AI Business OS Design Bible

Version: 1.0

---

# Design Mission

Build software that feels calm, premium and intelligent.

Every screen should communicate confidence, clarity and control.

---

# Product Personality

AI Business OS should feel:

- Calm
- Intelligent
- Professional
- Fast
- Minimal
- Trustworthy

Never noisy.

Never overwhelming.

---

# Visual Principles

## One Focus

Every screen has exactly one primary focus.

---

## Space

Whitespace is a feature.

Never fill empty space just because it exists.

---

## Typography

Large headlines.

Small supporting text.

Clear hierarchy.

---

## Motion

Animations should explain.

Never distract.

---

## Widgets

Every widget follows the same structure.

Header

↓

Content

↓

Footer (optional)

---

## Colors

Background

Surface

Border

Primary

Success

Warning

Danger

---

## Icons

Lucide React only.

No mixed icon styles.

---

## Radius

Use large rounded corners.

Everything should feel soft.

---

## Shadows

Very subtle.

Never heavy.

---

## Buttons

Primary

Secondary

Ghost

Danger

Only these four.

---

## Navigation

Sidebar is the heart of the product.

Users should always know where they are.

---

## Dashboard

The dashboard answers five questions:

What matters?

Who is working?

How are we performing?

What happened?

What should I do next?

---

## AI

AI should feel invisible.

Users should notice results.

Not technology.

---

# Premium Test

Before shipping any screen ask:

Would this look at home next to Linear?

Would this feel natural next to Apple software?

Would I proudly use this every day?

If the answer is no...

Improve it.
