# Domain Model

AI Business Operating System models a business instead of modeling software.

---

Company

↓

Goals

↓

Workflows

↓

Tasks

↓

Employees

↓

Integrations

↓

Business Results

---

## Company

Owns everything.

---

## Goal

Defines business objectives.

Created by humans.

Examples

- Launch Product
- Increase Revenue
- Publish Today's Content

---

## Workflow

Business process.

Transforms a Goal into executable Tasks.

---

## Task

Atomic unit of work.

Executed by AI employees.

---

## Employee

AI specialist.

Executes Tasks.

Never defines strategy.

---

## Department

Groups employees.

Coordinates business capabilities.

---

## Integration

External systems.

Instagram

Gmail

Stripe

Slack

Google Calendar

Shopify

---

## Business Brain

Coordinates Goals.

Never executes Tasks directly.

---

## Workflow Engine

Creates Tasks.

---

## Task Engine

Schedules work.

---

## Registry

Finds the right employee.

---

## AI Workforce

Executes work.

---

Everything starts with a Goal.

Everything ends with a Business Result.
